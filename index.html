About Us

Two Youngsters of KL University had a vision of Developing Product on Real time Problems, with the Technology and Innovation.


Nanduru Techsol was started on 10-06-2018 and registered legally as private limited on 5th oct 2018. This company was located in the heart of vijayawada. Ensuring the safety of the people is the company's main motto. Innovating things which keeps the people safe & sound and making them available at affordable prices is the company's main work. Other than this providing support/ incubation to budding startups is also dine by the company.


Our Products


NapLocks

AI device fixed to the vehicle to monitor the fatigue states of the driver. It is placed on the dashboard of the vehicle to evaluate the states of the driver. Whenever it finds out the driver is in drowsy state an alaram will be activated and the drivers seat cushion will be vibrated and an intimation will be given to the emergency contacts


AlcoSniffer

An IoT breath analyzing device fixed to the vehicle to analyze the alchohol of the driver. It is placed on the dashboard of the vehicle. It only allows the vehicle to be started when it finds out the driver is not in the drunken state. If it finds out the driver is in drunken state it won't allow the car to start.
